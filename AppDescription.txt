Charm Quark is a fast paced puzzle game inspired by the Large Hadron Collider.

- GAMEPLAY

·Match like colored particles by swiping the right side.

·Fire new particles by tapping the left side.

·Get bonus points for combos and extra matches.

·Keep the particles inside the detector!

- FEATURES
·3 Game Modes:
  Time Attack - How many points can you score before time runs out?
  Accelerator - Pieces will drop automatically.  Can you keep up?
  Practice - Learn the basics. Try out combo techniques.

·Dynamic background music.

·Gamecenter Leaderboards.

- SUPPORTED DEVICES
·iPhone 3GS, iPod Touch 3rd Gen or Later, iPad
·iOS 4.3 or later
