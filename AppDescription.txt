Charm Quark is a fast paced puzzle game inspired by the Large Hadron Collider.

GAMEPLAY:
·Match like colored particles by swiping the right side.
·Fire next particle by tapping the left side.
·Get bonus points for combos and extra matches.
·Keep the particles inside the detector!

THREE GAME MODES:
·Time Attack - How many points can you score before time runs out?
·Accelerator - Particles launch faster each level.  Can you keep up?
·Meditation - Learn the basics. Practice combo techniques.

FEATURES:
·Universal app with iPad and iPhone / iPod Touch support!
·Gamecenter leaderboards and achievements.

SUPPORTED DEVICES:
·iPhone 3GS, iPod Touch 3rd Gen or Later, iPad
·iOS 4.3 or later required.
